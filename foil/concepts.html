Concepts

There are a few general concepts that constantly appear at the higher levels, and which keep fencing fun. More importantly, they stave off frustration. Frustration is what you feel when you know you could've done better. A few simple rules of thumb can give you points you would not have otherwise earned, or at least make you sure you tried your best.
